Our Games

Battleground

Battleground, is a popular battle royale game. In it, up to 100 players parachute onto an island, scavenge for weapons, and fight to be the last person or team standing. It’s available on mobile devices.

Mobile Lords

Lords Mobile combines open-world RPG, real-time multiplayer strategy, and world-building mechanics. Players raise armies, forge alliances, and conquer enemies. The game features heroes, monsters, and familiars.

Survival Island

Survival Island is a survival game where players are stranded on a deserted island. They must gather resources, craft tools, and build shelter to survive. The game features a day-night cycle, weather system, and a vast open world.
